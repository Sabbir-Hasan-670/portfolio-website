About Me

My Journey

I am a Computer Science graduate with strong skills in Networking, Cybersecurity, and Full Stack Development. I have completed CCNA 200-301 training, gaining hands-on experience with routing, switching, VLANs, ACLs, NAT, and network troubleshooting using Packet Tracer and GNS3. In addition to networking, I possess knowledge of cybersecurity tools such as Metasploit, Nmap, and The Harvester, as well as programming languages including Python, Java, C/C++, and SQL. I have also built projects like a Restaurant Management System in Python and a Java-based Currency Converter. I hold certifications and training from Google, Udemy, and TCM Security in various areas, including Ethical Hacking, Penetration Testing, Digital Marketing, and Malware Analysis. Fluent in English, Bangla, and Hindi, I bring strong communication skills, teamwork, and a problem-solving mindset. I am seeking entry-level opportunities as a Network Engineer, Cybersecurity Analyst, or IT Support Engineer, where I can contribute my skills and continue learning.

Whether I am configuring network infrastructure, building dynamic web applications with Node.js and SQL, or designing corporate branding assets in Adobe Illustrator, my focus is always on delivering high-performance, secure, and visually striking experiences.
